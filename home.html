Privacy Policy

Bookmark Pro is designed with a "local-only" philosophy. Your data belongs to you and stays on your machine.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The extension handles the following data strictly to function:

  • Website Content: When you save a bookmark, we store the URL and page title.
  • Web History: We access the active tab's metadata only when you click the extension icon to help you save links faster.

2. Data Storage

All bookmarks and categories are stored locally in your browser's chrome.storage.local. No data is ever transmitted to a remote server or third party.

3. Data Control

You have full control over your data. You can delete individual bookmarks or categories at any time. Uninstalling the extension automatically wipes all locally stored data.

4. Security

Since data is stored locally, your information is as secure as your personal device.
